About the fundraiser

We're excited to be hosting the First Edition of Open Floor. All great ideas need a medium, and we are providing just that. Over two days we're going to host a variety of very accomplished speakers, in our Speakers List. They will take stage and either teach, share an experience, or moderate a discussion. Click here to check out our speakers. On the other end we're having an Open Floor where the house is open. Everyone is invited to pitch their craziest ideas within three minutes. There are no topics off limits, and we're leaving no stones unturned. We, at Open Floor, want to creat a space which celebrates intellegent dialogue. And, we need you to join us. The money raised from this event will go towards funding two free schools in Banglore and Calcutta.
